Real-Time Video Generation Benchmarks

Welcome to the ongoing Real-Time Video Comparison Project. Here we compare state-of-the-art models side-by-side across various inference steps to determine the true trade-off between visual fidelity and real-time generation speed on high-end hardware like the NVIDIA H100. We will continuously add new models as they are released.

About This Benchmark

For extensive details about the MemFlow and Helios-Distilled models, the testing methodology, and our findings on real-world throughput vs paper claims, please refer to the extensive details in the repository README.
